How to Play

The idea of the game is to follow the pattern created by the house crests.

1. One of the house crests will light up

2. Copy the game by clicking on the same house crest

3. The game will duplicate the first signal and light up a second house crest

4. Copy the game by clicking on both house crests in the order they were lit up

5. The game will add another signal by lighting up a third house crest

It is your job to copy the games signals

Good Luck!
